To estimate the distance to a galaxy:
  1. Rotate the circled galaxy (hold control and click + drag to the left or right) until the longest dimension of the galaxy is vertical
  2. Zoom in/out until the galaxy (not the surrounding circle!) just matches the height of the display
  3. Click on "ESTIMATE DISTANCE"
